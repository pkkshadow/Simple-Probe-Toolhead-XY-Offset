RETICLE FREE

Point the camera straight up at the nozzle. Drag the reticle onto a spot near center, then lock it — from here you only jog the printer, never the reticle. Nudge with ← ↑ → ↓ (hold Shift for coarse). Zoom centers on the reticle.

Method

  1. Aim camera up at the toolhead, focus on the nozzle, place & lock the reticle.
  2. Jog so the nozzle tip sits on the reticle. Enter that X/Y, Add sample. Repeat 2–3× for a tighter average.
  3. Jog so the center of the sensor face sits on the reticle. Enter X/Y, Add sample.
  4. Read the offset below and copy the config.
